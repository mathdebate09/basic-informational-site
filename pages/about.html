ABOUT OUR FAN CLUB

Learn about our history, mission, and the people behind the BVB09 Fan Club

OUR STORY

Founded in 2005, the BVB09 Fan Club was created by a group of passionate Borussia Dortmund supporters who wanted to create a community for fans to come together and celebrate their love for the club.

What started as a small gathering of 15 friends has now grown into one of the largest unofficial BVB fan clubs with over 5,000 members worldwide. We organize match viewings, trips to Signal Iduna Park, and various community events throughout the year.

Our mission is simple: to support Borussia Dortmund through thick and thin, to create a welcoming community for all BVB fans, and to embody the club's motto of "Echte Liebe" (True Love).

OUR HISTORY

2005

Fan club founded by 15 friends in a local pub in Dortmund

2008

Membership reaches 100 members

2011

First organized trip to a Champions League away match

2013

Fan club travels to Wembley for the Champions League final

2017

Membership reaches 1,000 members

2023

Fan club celebrates 18 years with over 5,000 members worldwide
